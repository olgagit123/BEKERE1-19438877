"One touch of nature makes the whole world kin"
-William Shakespeare
     It is proved, that the nature is the best treatment. To improve your bad mood sometimes it’s just enough to look at the picture or video of natural landscape. Of course it is best to look at the nature from your window. Also the nature contemplation helps you to concentrate, to recover from the illness. And if you decided to go to the nature the positive effect for your health will be even better. Nature creates the movement. When walking in the forest we do not notice that in fact we’re moving faster and burning more calories than inside. Spend only 5 minutes in the nature and you will feel that your mood is getting better. But after 15-20 minutes your life energy will increase. Moreover the blood pressure is going down and the cortisol concentration in blood is reducing. Cortisol is a hormone which is released in response to stress and can cause a headache. After two hours in the nature because of Natural killer cells number increase your immune system will get stronger. Walking in the nature facilitates social well-being and community feeling, we react more positive on other people and our mood is getting better very fast.
